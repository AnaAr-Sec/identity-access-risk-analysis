# Identity & Access Risk Analysis

## Objective
Analyze risks related to improper access management in organizational systems.

## Key Risks

### Excessive Privileges
Users may receive more permissions than required, increasing the risk of misuse or internal fraud.

### Lack of Access Reviews
Without periodic reviews, former employees or role changes may leave unnecessary permissions active.

### Weak Authentication Controls
Weak authentication mechanisms can allow unauthorized access to sensitive systems.

## Risk Impact

Improper access management can lead to:

- Data breaches
- Financial fraud
- Unauthorized system modifications
